Mu

Mu is an operating system for Life. It's based on the foundations of almost a decade of work on Micro, an API first development platform.

Today's operating systems were built in an iterative fashion to enable us as humans to complete tasks and do work, later repurposed for entertainment and a myriad of other social services. Yet none of these were created based on the values by which we live our lives or in a way we interact with the environment around us.

Mu rethinks the OS from the ground up. Primarily geared towards human interaction through text and speech rather than point and click. We're building it as a network first rather than something that just exists on your phone or computer. We'll have more to share soon.
