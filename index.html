Mu

Mu is an operating system for Life. It's based on the foundations of more than a decade of work on Micro, an API first development platform, and related projects.

Today's operating systems were built in an iterative fashion to enable us as humans to complete tasks and do work alone, later augmented by the internet and repurposed for entertainment and a myriad of other social services. Yet none of these were created based on the way in which we live our lives or how we interact with the environment around us.

Mu rethinks the OS from the ground up. Primarily geared towards human interaction through text and speech rather than than point and click. We're building it as a multiplayer first network instead of the old single user model. We'll have more to share on how to get involved soon.
